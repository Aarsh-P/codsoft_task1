50 years of serving communities

Our journey began in 1954 on a bustling street in Miami, Florida, where two passionate food enthusiasts opened the first Burger Delight. Inspired by the burgeoning American love for fast food and the classic drive-in experience, they set out to create the perfect burger. Their secret recipe, a blend of fresh ingredients and unique spices, quickly won the hearts of locals.

Fast forward to the late 1960s, a visionary named Jack Simmons discovered Burger Delight during a road trip across the South. Enthralled by the flavors and the community-centric atmosphere, Jack joined the team in 1970, bringing with him innovative ideas that would revolutionize the brand. Inspired by the iconic diners and burger joints of the era, he introduced the concept of the modern fast-food restaurant with a focus on quality and service.

By the early 1980s, Burger Delight had expanded beyond Miami, with locations sprouting up across the United States.
